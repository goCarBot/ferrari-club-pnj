## Positioning Copy
The Penn Jersey Region is proud to announce the 2026 Pennsylvania Concorso Ferrari. We are anticipating 100 Ferraris rolling in and over 250 attendees.

This year we will be parking our Ferraris on pavement and we have an onsite hotel available for our distant travelers.

Secure your spot now. This is a rain or shine event.
