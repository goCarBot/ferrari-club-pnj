About the Festival

The Garden State Ferrari Fall Festival is New Jersey's premier Ferrari celebration, held each autumn at one of the Garden State's most prestigious venues. Join fellow Ferrari enthusiasts for a day of beautiful cars, great company, and the stunning fall foliage of Princeton.

Event Highlights

A world-class celebration of Ferrari heritage, community, and the beauty of autumn in New Jersey.

🏎️

Ferrari-Only Show

An exclusive concours dedicated solely to Ferrari automobiles — from vintage classics to the latest models, all displayed on a pristine golf course setting.

🏆

IAC/PFA Judging

Cars are evaluated by certified judges following IAC and PFA standards, with awards recognizing excellence in preservation, restoration, and presentation.

⛳

Prestigious TPC Venue

Held at TPC Jasna Polana, one of New Jersey's finest private golf clubs — a stunning backdrop of manicured greens and autumn color in Princeton.

Featured Venue

One of New Jersey's finest private golf clubs, TPC Jasna Polana provides a stunning backdrop for our annual fall celebration. Set among the rolling hills of Princeton, the club's manicured grounds and elegant facilities create the perfect setting for showcasing the world's most beautiful automobiles.
