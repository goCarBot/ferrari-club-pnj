About Our Region

Three chapters. One passion.

Our Story

Penn-Jersey Region

The Penn-Jersey Region is comprised of three chapters: Eastern Pennsylvania, Western Pennsylvania, and New Jersey. We are a non-profit club with all income going to subsidize club activities and any surpluses donated to charities.

We have over 400 members with a very broad variety of Ferraris but you do not have to own one to be a member—you just have to have a passion for them.

What We Do

Club Activities

Social Activities

Our region hosts a diverse calendar of social events throughout the year—scenic rallies through Pennsylvania's countryside, technical sessions at premier garages, Formula 1 watch parties, Cars & Coffee gatherings, and exclusive track days at world-class circuits like Pocono Raceway and Watkins Glen.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or simply appreciate the artistry of Italian engineering, there's always an event that fits your style.

Concours Events

The region proudly hosts two premier Concours d'Elegance events each year: the Pennsylvania Concorso Ferrari in the spring and the Garden State Ferrari Fall Festival each autumn.

These judged events bring together the finest examples of Ferrari craftsmanship, from vintage classics to modern supercars, showcased at exceptional venues across our region.
