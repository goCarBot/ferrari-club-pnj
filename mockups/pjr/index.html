Who We Are

About Our Club

We are a non-profit club with all income going to subsidize club activities that consist of Concours, Rallies, Cars & Coffee, Tech Sessions and much more. We also have a history of supporting a number of very worthy charities.

We have over 400 members that own a wide range of Ferrari models that represent our favorite marque over the years. However, you do not have to own a Ferrari to be a member—you just have to have a passion for them!
