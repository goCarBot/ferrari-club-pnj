Membership Benefits

As a member of the Ferrari Club of America, you gain access to exclusive benefits from the national organization and the Penn-Jersey Region.

📰

Ferrari Market Letter

Exclusive subscription to the premier Ferrari market publication with pricing data, auction results, and expert analysis.

💰

Putnam Leasing

Preferred rates and terms on exotic and classic car financing through Putnam Leasing, the leader in specialty vehicle finance.

✈️

Moto Sports Travel

Curated travel packages to international motorsport events, factory tours, and driving experiences across Europe.

🛡️

Hagerty Insurance

Agreed-value classic and collector car insurance with flexible usage, no mileage restrictions, and dedicated claims support.

🔨

Mecum Auctions

Member-exclusive access and preferred bidding at Mecum collector car auctions across the country.

🏏️

Ferrari Dealership Locator

Direct connections to authorized Ferrari dealerships for sales, service, and exclusive member programs.

🛍️

Ferrari Store

Access to official Ferrari merchandise, apparel, and accessories through the FCA member store.
